Our History

Founded in 2008 by a small group of outdoor enthusiasts along the banks of the Niger River, Rafting Travels Co. began with a single inflatable raft and a shared vision—to bring people closer to nature through the thrill of whitewater adventures. At first, our trips were simple weekend excursions for friends and local explorers. Word quickly spread about the excitement and safety of our guided runs, and what began as a hobby transformed into a trusted rafting service.

Over the years, we have expanded our routes to include both gentle scenic floats for beginners and challenging rapids for thrill‑seekers. Our guides—many of whom have been with us since the early days—are certified professionals trained not just in navigation but also in first aid, river ecology, and customer care. Their expertise has helped us earn a reputation for safety, professionalism, and unforgettable experiences.

Today, Rafting Travels Co. operates year‑round and continues to introduce people from across the region—and the world—to the beauty and adventure of Nigeria’s rivers. Our mission remains the same: to connect people with nature, foster teamwork, and create memories that last a lifetime.
